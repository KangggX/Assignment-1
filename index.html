Welcome to my Portfolio

Hi, my name is Kang Xuan, a year 1 student studying in Ngee Ann Polytechnic for a Diploma in Immersive Media.

After graduating and receiving my GCE O Level results from my initial Secondary School, Teck Whye Secondary School, I was extremely relieved as my results were aligned with the "Cut Off" points for my dream course, Immersive Media, and there are many reasons as to why I am interested in this particular course.

One of the many reasons is that I enjoy playing video games at a daily basis. As a gamer, I often find myself trying to understand the video games I play on a deeper level such as the different mechanics available. I am also curious on how the game is developed from scratch and the amount of time the developers have put in to program and make the game's art.

By joining this course, I hope to get an answer to my curiousity on games as well as learning various UI, UX and 3D modelling skills from talented teachers over the course of 3 years. I am proud to be a student of Immersive Media and would like to pursue further education related to the course upon graduating.
